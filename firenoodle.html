Instruction

  • 1. Cook the instant noodles according to the package instructions and set aside.🍜
  • 2. In a bowl, mix together the gochujang🌶, soy sauce🧂, sugar🧂, vegetable oil🫒, garlic🧄, and sesame oil﹅﹅ (if using).
  • 3 Heat a pan over medium heat and add the sauce mixture. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • 4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan🍳 and stir until the noodles are coated with the sauce.

  • 5. Garnish with scallions😈 and sesame seeds and serve hot.
